Books

I belive reading is the best skill that we have. Apart from it being our main source of knowledge, reading reduces stress. Reading about something you enjoy or losing yourself in a good novel is an excellent way to relax. It can ease tension in your muscles and heart while letting your brain wander to new ideas and live in someone else's shoes.

Reading also has many scientifically proven health and social benefits:

  • improves brain connectivity.
  • increases your vocabulary and comprehension.
  • empowers you to empathize with other people.
  • aids in sleep readiness.
  • lowers blood pressure and heart rate.
  • fights depression symptoms.
  • prevents cognitive decline as you age

  • Limitless, by Jim Kiwk
  • Grit, by Angela Duckworth
  • Start somethind that matters, by Blake
  • The Art of War, by Sun Tsu
  • Infinite Game, by Simon Sinek
  • Make your bed, by William H. McRaven
